all of a sudden all my energy went down.So i was praying that something should happen atlest in alleppey.It was quite long journey not quite but too long journey.Finally,lunch time , i was checking my instagram feed and mom called me,But she does'nt know that i had mutton biryani with side of kabab's which was brought to me by fathers assistant yes off course you guessed it rite!!!.The train was passing through the hometown(Ambur,TN) of dad in Tamil-nadu.I some how managed him to bring the items i had .It was the Best biryani i ever had and also the kababs i think you guys should also try the "THE AMBUR BIRYANI".After having lunch i plugged in my ear pods and made my self comfortble.Stations are passing by diiferent different languages,cultures,temples these train journies are most beautiful journies in the whole world that's why i think most of the authors prefer train scenarios.WOW!! how cool is the weather it was wonderful.After passing stations,it was around 8 in the evening,the train had a halt in a town i don't remember the name.There i saw a vendor who is selling "Bannana Chips" so i thought that we had entered Kerala.I jumped out the train and after having some gibrish talks with him i finally brought my chips.I was enjoying my chips and dad passed by he saw me holding that chips packet and said "you could have get these chips in kerala because,its the famous place ".how innocent i am!!!.It was 4 in the morning we arrived that the station .we arranged a cab to the hotel,never ever forget that train journey in my life......
